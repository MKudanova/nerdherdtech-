NerdHerd Technologies Physical & Hardware Access Rules

Office Access

  • Office open: Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm
  • Use your badge to enter. Wear it at all times.
  • Visitors must check in at the front desk and wear a visitor badge.
  • No badge, no entry.
  • After hours: Only approved staff allowed.
  • Security cameras monitor all entries.
  • Don’t let anyone in behind you (“no tailgating”).
  • Report lost or stolen badges to IT.

Data Center Access

  • Only IT and managers can enter.
  • Badge and fingerprint required.
  • Access any time for approved staff; all entries logged.
  • Emergency access: IT must log and approve.
  • Vendors: Must be approved and escorted.
  • Server cabinets always locked.

USB and Portable Devices

  • Only use company-approved, encrypted USB drives.
  • Personal or unknown USB drives are not allowed.
  • USB ports are usually disabled. IT can approve exceptions.
  • If you find a USB drive, give it to IT—never plug it in.
  • Breaking these rules may get your access revoked.

Questions? Ask IT.
