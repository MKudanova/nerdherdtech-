PHYSICAL AND HARDWARE ACCESS POLICY

for NERDHEARD TECHNOLOGY

Welcome to the official access policy for NerdHerd Technologies employees. This page outlines our rules for physical entry, hardware access, and use of removable media in a way that keeps our workplace secure, efficient, and—let’s be honest—nerdy.

Office Suite Access Policy

  • Business Hours: Monday–Friday, 9:00am–5:00pm.
  • Entry: All employees must use their access badges.
  • After-Hours Access: Only approved staff with proper authorization. All after-hours entries are logged and monitored.
  • Security Tech: Electronic card readers, security cameras, and alarm systems protect all entry points.
  • Friendly Reminder: No “tailgating.” Always display badges. Report lost badges immediately.

Data Center Access Policy

  • Authorized Personnel Only: Access to the data center is limited to IT staff and designated managers. Entry requires both an access badge and biometric authentication./li>
  • 24/7 Access: Data center is accessible only to authorized personnel at all times; all entries/exits are logged.
  • Vendor Access: Vendors and non-employees require pre-approval and must be escorted by IT at all times.
  • Cabinet Security: Server racks are kept locked at all times. Keys held only by IT staff.
  • Emergency Access: Emergency entry is permitted for authorized staff only.

Portable Storage Device Policy

  • Approved Devices Only: Only company-issued, encrypted USB drives may be used. Personal USBs are strictly prohibited.
  • Enforcement: USB ports are disabled by default; exceptions require IT approval. Regular audits are performed.
  • If in Doubt: Never plug in an unknown USB device. Report it to IT immediately. Violations may lead to disciplinary action (and disappointed IT staff. Trust us, you dont want to disappoint the IT dep.).

For any questions about this policy, please contact the NerdHerd Tech IT department.
Thank you for keeping our office and data safe!
